Predictive Analysis Project

As part of our internship with Ismail Industries Pvt. Ltd, our team developed machine learning models to support predictive analytics in sales forecasting.

We were provided with 16 months of sales data, spanning from January 2024 to April 2025. Our models were trained on varying portions of this dataset and tested on the remaining data in order to evaluate performance and optimize for the best predictive results. Forecasts were then generated for the remaining months of 2025. To ensure robust performance, the models used time-series features such as monthly and quarterly lag variables, rolling averages and variances. This allowed us to capture demand patterns more accurately and produce actionable insights.

Limitations

Time Constraint

Our internship spanned 8 weeks, during which we encountered several challenges, including data inconsistencies and hardware complications, that had to be resolved before we could properly begin our project.

Constraints in Dataset

We were provided with a relatively small dataset spanning 16 months, which led to inconsistent forecasting results and limited the modeling techniques we could apply. Additionally, the dataset contained issues such as missing values and negative entries that further impacted data quality..
